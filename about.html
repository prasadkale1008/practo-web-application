About Us

Welcome to the Practo App! We are dedicated to simplifying the process of managing medical records and lab tests for individuals. Our application offers a seamless platform for booking lab tests, accessing health records, and keeping track of medical history.

Our mission is to make healthcare accessible and organized for everyone, allowing individuals to focus on their health without the hassle of paperwork and complicated booking systems.

We offer a range of services, including blood tests, Diabetes tests, X-ray tests, and much more.
